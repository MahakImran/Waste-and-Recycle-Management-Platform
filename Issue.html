WHAT CAN AND CAN'T BE RECYCLED?

Recycling helps conserve natural resources, reduce landfill waste, and lower carbon emissions. However, it's important to know which materials can be recycled and which cannot.

Materials that can be Recycled include:

  • Paper (newspapers, cardboard, office paper)
  • Metals (aluminum cans, steel cans)
  • Certain plastics (like PET bottles and containers labeled with recycling codes 1-2)

Materials that can't be Recycled include:

  • Plastic bags
  • Food-contaminated items (pizza boxes, food wrappers)
  • Styrofoam

By following local recycling guidelines, we can prevent contamination and ensure more materials are recycled properly.
