How Recycling Helps Fight Climate Change?

  • Energy Savings:

    Recycling aluminum, for instance, saves up to 95% of the energy required to make new aluminum from bauxite.

  • Low Carbon Footprints:

    Recycling paper and plastics instead of producing new ones cuts down on the carbon emissions that contribute to global warming.
